Who We Are

Velocity Vortex is a dedicated team of students passionate about engineering, design, and innovation. We strive to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of F1 in Schools, combining creativity with technical expertise to build competitive and innovative race cars.

What We Do

At Velocity Vortex, we design, build, and race F1 in Schools cars. Our team spends countless hours perfecting our designs, ensuring they meet all regulations and perform at their best. We are committed to excellence and innovation, constantly seeking to improve and learn.
