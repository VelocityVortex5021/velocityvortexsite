Who We Are

Velocity Vortex is a high-performance team of student engineers and designers united by a passion for innovation and excellence. As competitors in STEM in Schools, we blend creativity, technical skill, and relentless problem-solving to push the boundaries of what’s possible in miniature race car design. Our team is driven by a shared commitment to precision, sustainability, and teamwork—values that define both our process and our results. From the drawing board to the race track, we strive to represent the future of engineering, one design at a time.

What We Do

At Velocity Vortex, we don’t just build cars—we engineer solutions. Our process begins with cutting-edge CAD design and aerodynamics modeling, ensuring every curve and component is optimized for speed, efficiency, and compliance with F1 in Schools regulations. We prototype, test, and refine our designs using advanced materials and manufacturing techniques, collaborating closely with mentors and industry professionals to bring our vision to life. Beyond the car, we focus on project management, branding, and community engagement. Our trade display and digital platforms—like this website—are designed to showcase not only our technical achievements but also the story behind our team: the challenges we’ve overcome, the skills we’ve developed, and the innovation that drives us forward. Whether it’s on the track or in the workshop, we’re always learning, adapting, and aiming for excellence.
